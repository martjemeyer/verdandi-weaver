Stay near

A slow letter, kept near

Slow reflections, new frameworks, future gatherings, and quiet things worth sharing. Sent when there is something to say — not because a calendar said so.

What you'll receive

Quiet, infrequent, never funnelled

Cadence

A letter every few weeks

Whenever a reflection or framework is ready. Sometimes twice in a month; sometimes once in two. Never daily.

Shape

One long thought, well-held

A short reflection, often paired with a quote, a question, and a single onward thread into the archive.

First news

Gatherings, gently

Circles and retreats are announced here, weeks before they open elsewhere. With small numbers, they often fill from this letter alone.

What it isn't

Not a funnel

No countdowns, no scarcity language, no automation that gets faster the longer you stay. If something is for sale, it is named simply, once.
